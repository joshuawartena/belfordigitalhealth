## 1. The brand at a glance

**Chem-Dry®** is a carpet and upholstery cleaning franchise. The flagship trademark line: **"The Healthier, Deeper Carpet Cleaner®"** *(note: this mark is now registered ® per Section Five; the editorial style guide still shows ™)*. Core proprietary cleaner: **The Natural®**. Core process: **Hot Carbonating Extraction (HCE)**.

The brand promise hinges on three ideas: *cleaner, healthier homes*; carbonation (not soapy detergents); and using less water than traditional steam cleaning.

### Target customer
- Female homeowner, **45+**, household income **$100k+**
- Often has **children and/or pets**

### Voice and tone
- **Professional, but human, friendly, and familiar.** Communication should feel like it's coming from a real person, not a faceless corporation.
- Clear and concise. No fluff words. Educate and inform without overwhelming.
- Consumer-facing language — **never** technical industry jargon.
- **Active voice** in most situations.
- **Contractions are encouraged** — write like you speak.
- Shorter words and shorter sentences.
- Default to **AP Style** and **Merriam-Webster** for anything not specified.

## 3. Typography

The brand specifies **six faces** across three roles:

### Headlines (print & web)
- **Arial Bold**
- **Futura Bold**
- **Bree Semi-Bold Oblique** *(used for italic display work — e.g., "The Healthier, Deeper Carpet Cleaner" tagline)*

### Body copy & supporting text (print & web)
- **Arial**
- **Futura Book**

### Legacy tagline only — "Drier. Cleaner. Healthier.®"
- **Garamond Book** or **Garamond Bold** *(only used when this older approved tagline appears with the logo — not for general headline or body use). The "Drier. Cleaner. Healthier.®" tagline itself is being retired — see logo transitions below.*

### Franchise name
- **Futura Bold or Arial Bold only** (Section 2).
- Always upper/lower case, never ALL CAPS.
- Must be completely horizontal (no tilts or arcs).
- Must be **50–70% of the largest logo's height** when paired with the Chem-Dry logo.

### Working notes
- These are the only sanctioned typefaces. **Do not substitute** Inter, Open Sans, Roboto, etc.
- Bree is the brand's only italic display face — it carries the tagline and headline italics. When Bree isn't licensed in the project, the tagline must be set as artwork from the official logo file, not as live italic Arial/Futura.
- For web work where Futura isn't licensed, Arial is the safe fallback (it's already approved). Avoid web-font substitutes for Futura unless the user signs off.

## 6. Brand-specific language rules

Franchises can be **fined up to $2,000 and/or have their franchise terminated** for non-compliance, so these apply to anything created for Chem-Dry.

### Trademark mechanics

- **Chem-Dry can never be possessive.** Never write "Chem-Dry's". Use instead:
  - "The Chem-Dry process"
  - "The method used by Chem-Dry"
  - or a similar workaround
- **Taglines can't be dropped into a sentence as ordinary prose** — they must be cited as taglines. ❌ *"Chem-Dry is the Healthier, Deeper carpet cleaner."* ✅ *"Call Chem-Dry — The Healthier, Deeper Carpet Cleaner®."*
- **The Natural®** always carries the ® mark.

### HCE (Hot Carbonating Extraction)

**Required statement** (must appear on all print and online marketing materials about carpet cleaning):

> *The Chem-Dry proprietary Hot Carbonating Extraction (HCE) cleaning process provides a deeper, longer lasting clean for a healthier home.*

Phone conversations must convey the same information.

**Approved HCE terms:**
- "Hot Carbonating Extraction cleaning process"
- "proprietary" is always allowed before the term; ❌ **"patented" is not.**

**HCE residue language:**
- ❌ Never say HCE leaves behind **"no residue"** or **"zero residue"**.
- ✅ Use **"no dirt-attracting residue"**, **"leaves no sticky or dirt-attracting residue"**, **"leaves no soapy residue"**, or **"virtually residue-free"**.

**HCE water-savings claims:**
- ❌ Never state the percentage as an absolute.
- ✅ Always use a modifier: **"about 80% less"**, **"approximately 80% less"**, **"nearly 80% less"**.

**Statements you CAN make about HCE:**
- "The Chem-Dry Hot Carbonating Extraction process uses about 80% less water than typical steam cleaning, so carpets dry in hours not days."
- "Our Hot Carbonating Extraction process works to extract deep down into your carpets without leaving behind soapy residue."
- "Our revolutionary carpet cleaning process uses carbonation and ingredients copied from Mother Nature for its core carbonating cleaning solution, The Natural®."

**Statements about the CTS truckmount:**
- "The CTS truckmount — hot water extraction with the power of carbonation."
- "Chem-Dry offers the most powerful, deepest cleaning system for maximum soil removal."
- "As a leader in the cleaning industry, Chem-Dry has taken its deep-cleaning and fast-drying process to the next level with the extraction power of the CTS truckmount system."

**CTS Flex Systems (CTS4800, CTS4.8, CTS400, CTS403) — bonus claims:**
- "State-of-the-Art Technology."
- "Cutting Edge Technology."
- "Most advanced cleaning technology in the industry."
- "New, latest, revolutionary."

### The Natural® — claims to AVOID

The core cleaner **The Natural®** may **NEVER** be described as:
- "all natural" *(it contains a chelating agent for water-softening)*
- "environmentally safe"
- "environmentally responsible"
- "green certified"

Also: you may **not** say "Chem-Dry is natural" or "The Chem-Dry process is natural" — only **The Natural® product / solution** can be called natural.

### The Natural® — APPROVED language (use exact wording)

- "Unlike other carpet cleaning products, The Natural contains NO soaps, detergents, or fragrances."
- "The Natural® doesn't rely on soapy detergents or harsh chemicals, but rather, uses carbonation to help lift dirt from carpets."
- "Only Chem-Dry cleans with The Natural®."
- "Chem-Dry uses a proprietary core cleaner, The Natural®."
- "The Natural® is made from ingredients copied from mother nature."
- "The Natural® refreshes and deep cleans your carpets without harsh chemicals."
- "The Natural® uses the power of carbonation for a deeper clean."
- "Our core cleaner, The Natural®, uses carbonation for a better clean and a healthier home."
- "Chem-Dry is a revolutionary carpet cleaning process that uses carbonation and ingredients copied from Mother Nature to make your carpets fresh and clean again."
- "The exclusive Chem-Dry process generates natural CO2 to produce millions of tiny, effervescent bubbles which penetrate the carpet fiber, dissolve dirt and stains quickly, and act as tiny propellants that actually lift dirt to the surface for extraction."

### Competitive / comparative claims

When describing what other cleaning methods do, **use "may" or "can"**:
- ✅ "May take days to dry."
- ✅ "Can cause mold and mildew."
- ❌ Don't make absolute claims about competitors.

**Stain removal language:**
- ✅ "If we can't get the stain out, no one can."
- ✅ "Removes virtually all stains" / "Removes most stains."
- ❌ Don't say Chem-Dry "removes all stains."

**Brand-size claims:**
- ✅ "Over 3,500 Chem-Dry Franchises Worldwide"
- ✅ "Chem-Dry is the #1 rated carpet cleaning franchise."
- ✅ "Chem-Dry is the world's largest carpet cleaning company."
- ✅ "Chem-Dry is the world's leading carpet cleaning company."
- ❌ Don't claim to be the largest Chem-Dry franchise in your state or area.

### CTA / approved button copy patterns
- "Schedule Today"
- "Call Chem-Dry"
- "Time for spring cleaning? We'll handle the carpet!" (campaign-level)
- "Eliminate pet urine odors for good with Chem-Dry carpet cleaning" (campaign-level)

## 9. Healthy Home / Allergen study claims & disclaimers

### Required full disclaimer (use exactly as written, with leading asterisk)

> *\*Based on results of studies conducted by independent laboratories of the Chem-Dry HCE (Hot Carbonating Extraction) and P.U.R.T. (Pet Urine Removal Treatment). Allergens tested were dog and cat dander and dust mite matter. Pet odor results based on testing with the most common odor sources found in dog and cat urine. Figures are an average across multiple tests.*

- Must appear on **every web page and marketing piece** where study results / Healthy Home claims appear.
- Disclaimer always begins with an asterisk.
- No other single asterisks may appear in the same piece (asterisks tie unambiguously to this disclaimer).
- Minimum 8-point font on print; legible size relative to medium.
- For Pay-Per-Click / radio / space-constrained mediums where the disclaimer can't fit, must link or direct the consumer to a page where the full study and disclaimer live (e.g., the franchise's Healthy Home page).

### Carpet/Upholstery-specific disclaimer
> *\*Removes allergens from non-living sources, such as dog and cat dander and dust mite matter. Based on results from a study conducted by an independent lab using the Chem-Dry Hot Carbonating Extraction process. Figures are an average across multiple homes.*

### P.U.R.T.-specific disclaimer
> *\*Based on results of a study conducted by an independent laboratory of the Chem-Dry P.U.R.T. (Pet Urine Removal Treatment) process. Odor results based on testing with the most common odor sources found in dog and cat urine. Figures are an average across multiple tests.*

## 10. Section Six — global Do/Don't statement list

The official rulebook's master cheat-sheet:

### ❌ Do NOT use
- "#1" (e.g., "the No. 1 carpet cleaning franchise")
- "10 BILLION SQ FT. of carpet is installed annually by home and business owners and **all** of it had to be cleaned"
- "Carpet cleaning is experiencing historic growth"
- The full "unique Hot Carbonating Extraction..." paragraph that claims "cleaner and healthier for longer than any other method"
- "We are proud to carry the seal of approval for our process from the Carpet and Rug Institute" *(CRI seal is discontinued)*
- "practically eliminating the risk of mold and mildew"
- "additional services like... sanitizing floor coverings"
- "Disinfect / sanitize" *(generic claims)*
- "Removes/reduces allergens" *(missing "non-living")*
- "Eliminates non-living allergens"
- "100% nontoxic" / "nontoxic"
- "safe and nontoxic for kids and pets"
- "doesn't leave behind sticky or dirt-attracting residue" *(wrong residue phrasing — see HCE rules)*
- "Because this product is natural, chemical- and toxin-free, it is the perfect solution for people with allergies"
- "Chem-Dry cleans and protects every surface in the home and workplace"
- "doesn't hold excess water that can breed mold and mildew within four or five hours after cleaning"
- "at the **molecular** level"
- "This high-pressure suction can damage the carpet" *(re: traditional steam cleaning)*
- "Green" / "It's green"

### ✅ Allowed alternatives
- "Top ranked" (e.g., "a top ranked franchise system")
- "Billions of square feet of carpet is installed annually in homes and businesses. That's a lot of carpet that needs to be cleaned!"
- "This process allows carpets to dry more quickly than traditional steam cleaning and makes carpets clean and healthy. This gives us an edge in the industry."
- "Chem-Dry has built the largest and most trusted carpet cleaning brand in the country."
- P.U.R.T. "Removes pet urine odors from carpets."
- "Nonliving allergens" / "Removes/reduces non-living allergens"
- "with no harsh chemicals" / "meaning it has no harsh chemicals"
- "Chem-Dry is an expert at cleaning and protecting surfaces in your home and workplace"
- "uses 80% less water than traditional steam cleaning, reducing the risk of mildew growth after cleaning"
- "at the **microscopic** level"
- "Dust Mite Anti-Allergen: ... powerful active ingredients proven effective against **non-living** household dust mite allergens."

### General green-claims principles (FTC-aligned)
- No broad/sweeping "green" claims.
- All environmental benefits must be specific and substantiated.
- No "free-of" claims if more than trace amounts of the substance are present, if the substance has no association to the product, or if an alternative with similar environmental effect is used.
- No "non-toxic" claims without competent and reliable scientific evidence for both (1) people and (2) the environment.

### Endorsement rules
- Endorsements must reflect the endorser's honest opinions / experience.
- Consumer endorsements alone don't substantiate claims — independent substantiation is required, or the typical-consumer experience must be disclosed and substantiated.
- Material connections (payment, consideration, employment) **must be disclosed**.

## 11. Franchise identity & co-branding (Section 2)

### Required on all Branded Materials
- **"INDEPENDENTLY OWNED AND OPERATED"** — must appear prominently and clearly visible on every Branded Material. The **only** exception is digital advertising, where the ad must link to a page/site that contains this phrase.
- **Area served** — every Branded Material must state the franchise's authorized area. Must be of significant size and clearly presented.
- **Full and correct franchise name** — exactly as authorized by BFG (e.g., *Chem-Dry by Jake*, *Capitol Chem-Dry*). Upper/lower case, never ALL CAPS, fully horizontal, 50–70% of the largest logo height.

### Forbidden framings
- ❌ "The authorized Chem-Dry franchise in [area]" or "the only authorized Chem-Dry franchise in [area]".
- ❌ "We Accept All Chem-Dry Coupons" or similar.

### Domains
- All domains promoting Chem-Dry services **must be approved by and registered through BFG** (WMS: 844-759-7646). Franchisees may not register their own; vendors may not register on behalf of franchisees.
- Each franchise gets one free `chem-dry.net/[franchisename]` subdomain.
- Each franchise may have **one main domain**; additional domains must be assigned to a franchise number.
- Domains containing "Chem-Dry," "cd," or part of the franchise name must include the **full franchise name (or approved abbreviation)**. Examples:
  - `advantagechemdry.com` / `advantagecd.com`
  - `chemdryseattle.com` / `chemdryofseattle.com`
  - `acechemdrycarpetcleaning.com`
- ❌ No generic Chem-Dry domains. ❌ No BFG trademarks in third-party domain registrations. ❌ No location references for areas a franchise doesn't service. ❌ Nothing predatory toward other franchisees.

### Marketing scope
- A franchise's advertising **must service a major portion of its franchised area and be tailored to circulate within that area only**.
- Direct mail: only to ZIP codes within the franchised area.
- Digital marketing: targeted only to ZIP codes within the franchised area.
- Out-of-area callers responding to ads must be referred to the appropriate franchise or to **1-800-CHEM-DRY (1-800-243-6379)** without compensation for the referral.

### "Serving areas" page rules (digital)
- Every website or landing page must state "Serving [the Franchised Area]."
- Specific community / city names and ZIP codes may be listed only with BFG approval and territory verification.
  - Format: bullet list, near bottom of page, **6pt minimum**, never the same color as the background.
- Required disclaimer below such lists:
  > *"The service provider is subject to service only within contractual boundaries. The service provider may not be able to service all portions of the listed areas due to contractual limitations."*

### Co-branding with non-Chem-Dry businesses
- Permitted only if businesses remain clearly distinct and separate (e.g., Chem-Dry on one side of a postcard, the other brand on the reverse).

### Cooperative advertising
- Two types: **general** (all area franchisees invited) and **limited** (subset invited). Limited co-ops require joint service areas — they cannot be used to extend reach.

## 12. Mediums (Section 4)

### Coupons — minimum required content
- Offer / discount
- Business name and/or phone / website
- Specific service details
- Expiration date (xx/xx/xx)
- Standard disclaimers:

  - "Minimum Charges May Apply"
  - "Subject to Availability"

### Digital
- **PPC ads:** if a Chem-Dry trademark is referenced, the full franchise name must be in the ad. Improper dynamic keyword insertion is prohibited. Listing the serving area or "Independently Owned & Operated" inline isn't required in short PPC creative.
- **Display ads:** must show the Chem-Dry logo, full franchise name, and link to a site/landing page with serving area + "Independently Owned & Operated."
- **Social media:** full franchise name (or approved abbreviation) in account name and profile; "Independently Owned & Operated" in profile description. Avoid using business accounts for opinions outside the franchise's services.
- **Groupon / LivingSocial / discount platforms:** **strongly discouraged** — they damage the brand. Pre-approval required from `marketing@belfrangroup.com`.
- **SEO:** BFG-preferred vendor recommended. Questions: `marketing@chemdry.com`.

### Yellow Pages
- Verify the directory circulates within the franchised area. Instruct the rep to keep the ad out of out-of-area books. Show franchise name and area served prominently.

- All equipment must visibly carry the Chem-Dry name and be well-maintained.

When building Chem-Dry web/digital work, drop these in a `:root` block:

```css
:root {

  --cd-purple:         #9A4E9E;  /* PMS 513 */
  --cd-green-bright:   #41AD49;  /* PMS 361 */

  /* Neutrals (web type) */
  --cd-warm-gray:      #A2958A;  /* PMS 7530 C — body */
  --cd-warm-gray-dark: #8A7967;  /* PMS 4090 C — secondary headings */
  --cd-white:          #FFFFFF;
  --cd-black:          #111111;

  /* Type stack */
  --cd-font-headline:  "Futura", "Arial", "Helvetica", sans-serif;
  --cd-font-headline-italic: "Bree", "Futura", "Arial", sans-serif; /* tagline display */
  --cd-font-body:      "Arial", "Helvetica", sans-serif;
}
```

**Rules of thumb when applying:**
- Headlines: Chem-Dry Blue **or** Chem-Dry Green. Avoid pure black.
- Body copy on light backgrounds: Chem-Dry Warm Gray, not black.
- Secondary headings on light backgrounds: Chem-Dry Dark Warm Gray.
- CTAs: solid Chem-Dry Green background, white text, generous padding.
- Avoid gradients. The brand is flat, photographic, and clean.
- Every Branded Material needs the franchise name (50–70% of logo height), area served, and "Independently Owned & Operated" — bake these into your template.
